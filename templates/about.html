What is Heal Hub?

A blended wellness, support, and care-coordination platform

Heal Hub brings together healing-through-stories, therapist discovery, moderated community support, safety education, mood tools, and the original hospital operations engine in one unified experience.

Why this matters

People need both emotional support and practical care pathways

A stronger health experience helps people feel less alone, supports safer choices, and makes it easier to move from reflection to real support when they need it.

How AI fits in

Soft recommendations plus explainable system logic

AI in Heal Hub helps with supportive next-step guidance and care coordination. It does not diagnose from movie preferences or turn emotional patterns into harmful conclusions.

Hackathon value

Built to feel alive, warm, and demo-ready immediately

Seeded stories, bookings, posts, replies, and safety resources make the product feel complete on first load while preserving the existing hospital dashboard for deeper technical demos.
