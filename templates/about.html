What is HealHub?

A smart hospital network coordination system

HealHub receives patient intake data, scores clinical urgency, evaluates live operational constraints, and recommends the best next action: treat immediately, wait safely, or redirect to another hospital.

Why high-value health systems matter

Healthcare quality depends on operations too

Strong health systems do more than diagnose correctly. They minimize avoidable delays, match limited resources to the highest-need patients, and reduce overload before it cascades across facilities.

How AI works here

Explainable scoring plus live resource reasoning

HealHub combines rule-based triage scoring with system-level prioritization. It weighs symptoms, severity, oxygen level, heart rate, blood pressure, age, arrival mode, chronic disease, pregnancy, and capacity pressure to rank every patient.

Why this matters for Nepal

Built for constrained healthcare environments

In Nepal, referral bottlenecks, limited ICU slots, and uneven hospital capacity can create dangerous delays. HealHub shows how digital intelligence can coordinate existing infrastructure more effectively.
