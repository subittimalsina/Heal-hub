Wellness support

HealHub now supports mental and physical recovery alongside hospital operations.

The same platform that coordinates triage, capacity, and routing can also support patient recovery with simple wellness guidance after care decisions are made.

Mood awareness Lifestyle tracking Recovery suggestions

Why it matters

Care does not stop when operations are optimized.

  • Patients can check mood, stress, sleep, and energy in one quick flow.
  • Recovery support stays lightweight and easy to demo in a hackathon setting.
  • Wellness guidance complements the hospital dashboard without changing core logic.
  • Healing feels more human when emotional and physical recovery are both acknowledged.

Recovery pillars

Four simple ways HealHub can support recovery after treatment decisions.

These additions stay intentionally simple and fit the current product style without changing the existing hospital operations workflow.

01 Mood

Mood Analysis

Track emotions and stress patterns so recovery pressure becomes easier to notice early.

02 Habits

Lifestyle Tracking

Monitor sleep, hydration, food, and movement as part of a balanced recovery routine.

03 Support

Healing Suggestions

Recommend breathing, music, meditation, and journaling when a patient needs a calmer day.

04 Connection

Human Connection

Remind patients to connect with loved ones so recovery keeps a human support layer.
